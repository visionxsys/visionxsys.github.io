GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Service-specific answers to the things clients ask us most.

How long does a typical project take? +
Timelines depend on scope. A landing page can be done in 5–7 days; a full web app or AI integration typically takes 3–8 weeks. We agree on clear milestones upfront so you always know where we are.
Do you offer revisions? +
Yes. Every package includes revision rounds. Starter gets 2, Pro gets 4, and Enterprise gets unlimited revisions within the agreed scope.
How do payments work? +
We operate on a 50% upfront, 50% on delivery model. For larger projects, we can split into milestone-based payments. We accept bank transfers and PayPal.
Can I request a custom package? +
Absolutely. Contact us and describe your project — we'll build a tailored quote that fits your exact needs and budget.
What tech stack do you use for web development? +
We primarily use React, Next.js, and Node.js for modern web apps. For simpler sites we use semantic HTML/CSS/JS. We can also work with your existing stack if required.
Will my site be mobile-responsive? +
Always. Every site we build is fully responsive and tested across multiple devices and browsers. Mobile-first is our default approach.
Do you handle hosting and deployment? +
Yes. We can deploy to Vercel, Netlify, AWS, or your preferred host. We'll set up CI/CD pipelines, SSL, and domain configuration as needed.
Can you redesign my existing website? +
Absolutely. We specialise in redesigns. We'll audit your current site, identify conversion problems, and deliver a modern, high-performing replacement.
Which AI models do you use for chatbots? +
We primarily use GPT-4o via the OpenAI API, but we also have experience with Claude, Gemini, and open-source models like LLaMA depending on your requirements and budget.
Can the chatbot be trained on my business data? +
Yes. We implement RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to train the chatbot on your docs, FAQs, and product info. The bot will answer accurately from your own knowledge base.
Where can the chatbot be deployed? +
Anywhere — your website, WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, Slack, or any platform with a webhook or API. We build custom integrations for your stack.
Is my data secure with an AI integration? +
We follow OWASP best practices and use encrypted communication. We never store sensitive data beyond what's needed. Enterprise plans include full data residency control.
Do you work in Figma only, or other tools too? +
Figma is our primary tool. We also use Adobe Illustrator for logo and brand identity work, and After Effects or Rive for animations.
Will I get the source files? +
Yes. Upon final payment you receive all source files — Figma files, layered PSDs, vector AI files, and export-ready assets in any format you need.
Can you create a full brand identity? +
Yes. Our Enterprise design plan includes logo, colour palette, typography system, brand guidelines, social media kit, and UI component library.
Do you create explainer or product demo animations? +
Yes! Our 2D animation service covers explainer videos, product demos, SaaS onboarding flows, and social media motion graphics — all with professional voiceover coordination if needed.
